Adding, modifying and classifying tetramers

Adding a tetramer

Press the 'a' key and left click the mouse in the approximate position you want the tetramer to appear. The position of the tetramer can be moved one pixel at a time using the four arrow keys on the keyboard. The rotation is controlled by the mouse wheel. Once you are satisfied with the position press the 's' key to save the tetramer position

Modifying a tetramer

First, identify the tetramer by pressing the 'm' key and left click on the desired tetramer, which will change colour to white (black if you change the palette). To alter the position and orientation of the tetramer use the four arrow keys and the mouse wheel. Press the 's' key once finished

Delete a Tetramer

To delete a tetramer, use the 'm' key and left click to highlight it, then use the 'd' key and it will be erased - Warning - this cannot be undone!

Valid Add/Modify keys

EscImmediately stop addition or modification
Arrow keys
(←, →, ↑ and ↓)		Move the tetramer 1 pixel in that direction
Mouse wheelRotate tetramer
dDelete the added tetramer
sSave tetramer after addition

Classify a Tetramer

To classify a tetramer, press the 'c' key and left click on the desired tetramer to highlight it, then use one of the following keys to classify it.

EscImmediately stop classification
b'both'
c'checkerboard'
i'isolated'
s'side-by-side'
u'unclassified'

Once the key is pressed the tetramer is classified
